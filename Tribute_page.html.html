A Tribute to APJ Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a reowned scientist, known for hsi work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first stellite launch vechicle (SLV-3)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
